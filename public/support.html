Frequently asked questions
How do I create a roadmap?
After signing in, complete the onboarding steps — choose your topic, skill level, goal, and daily time. Wintrail will automatically generate a personalised learning roadmap for you. You can also create additional roadmaps by tapping New Roadmap on the roadmaps screen.
Can I have multiple roadmaps?
Yes! You can create multiple roadmaps for different topics or goals. Switch between them from the Roadmaps screen. Each roadmap maintains its own progress, phases, and tasks independently.
How do I delete a roadmap?
Open the Roadmaps screen and find the roadmap you want to remove. Tap the options menu (three dots) on the roadmap card, then select Delete. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
My data disappeared — what do I do?
First, ensure you are signed in with the same email address you used when creating your account — your data is tied to your account, not the device. If you are signed in and data is still missing, try pulling down to refresh the screen. If the problem persists, please contact us at Sanjaykrishnanjv@gmail.com and we will help restore your data.
How do I delete my account?
To permanently delete your account and all associated data, email us at Sanjaykrishnanjv@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete my account" and include your registered email address. We will process the deletion within 7 business days.
How does the AI mentor work?
The AI mentor is powered by Groq AI (Llama 3.3 70B), a large language model that understands your learning context. It knows your current roadmap, topic, and progress — so you can ask it questions like "explain Phase 2 to me", "what should I focus on today?", or "give me a quiz on this topic." Responses are generated in real time and tailored to your learning journey. Note: AI responses are for guidance purposes and may not always be 100% accurate — always verify important information independently.
