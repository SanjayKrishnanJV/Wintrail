Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 26, 2026
Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data Wintrail collects, how it is used, and your rights over your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Wintrail, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: Your email address and display name provided at sign-up.
  • Learning preferences: Your chosen topic, skill level, learning goal, and daily time commitment gathered during onboarding.
  • AI conversation history: Messages you send to the Mentor AI feature and the responses generated.
  • Study progress: Task completion, streak data, hours studied, and milestone achievements.
  • Roadmap data: The personalised learning roadmaps generated for you, including phases, tasks, and resources.

We do not collect payment information, device identifiers for advertising, or sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Personalise your AI learning experience and generate tailored roadmaps.
  • Track and display your study progress and streak history.
  • Provide context to the AI mentor so it can give relevant, personalised guidance.
  • Restore your data when you sign in on a new device.
  • Improve the quality and accuracy of Wintrail over time.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Third-Party Services

Wintrail relies on the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — We use Supabase for secure cloud storage and authentication. Your account and roadmap data are stored in Supabase databases. Supabase is GDPR-compliant. See Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Groq AI (Llama 3.3 70B) — AI-generated responses in the Mentor and roadmap features are processed by Groq. Messages you send to the mentor may be transmitted to Groq's API for inference. Groq does not use these inputs to train models. See Groq Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated personal data — including your roadmaps, progress, and conversation history — will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Portability: Export your roadmap and progress data in JSON format from within the app (Settings → Export Data).

To exercise any of these rights, email us at Sanjaykrishnanjv@gmail.com.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Data at rest is stored securely via Supabase's encrypted database infrastructure.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

7. Children's Privacy

Wintrail is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at Sanjaykrishnanjv@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective date" at the top of this page. Continued use of Wintrail after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
